Course Curriculum

Our live online internship program provides students and professionals exposure to the practical aspects of psychology and counselling therapy. Psychology students can get exposure to real life cases and build an understanding of how to move forward with diagnosis, rapport building, and right therapeutic treatments. It is well structured into seven modules which include :-

1. Establishing relationship with the client : This module will help you understand how to create a rapport with the client and also attain information about their life to understand their cognition and behaviours. It will include rapport building, intake session, setting goals etc.

2. Probing skills and skill of silence : This module will equip you to learn what questions to ask , when to ask and when to remain silent and allow the client to reflect. The topics will also include types of probing, magic questions etc.

3. Assessments : This module will introduce various scientifically structured assessments to the trainees which can be extremely helpful in either making the diagnosis or understanding the clients behavioural patterns. It will include case studies and role play.

4. Understanding therapeutic models and framework: A theory and a therapeutic model acts as a roadmap that guides therapists through the process of understanding clients and bringing clarity in them. This module will include In depth understanding of cognitive-behavioural model and Trauma-informed approach ( 3 E’s of trauma, 4 R’s of trauma and the care therapeutic framework)

5. Formulating treatment plan : This will include session wise understanding of how to formulate a treatment plan and share it with your client. ( psycho-education, revisiting goals, recording progress, discussing the number of the sessions, interventions to be applied, deciding the pace of the therapy sessions, preparing for termination. managing crisis situation)

6. Therapeutic techniques : 6 therapy tools and interventions will be introduced which is in alignment with the therapeutic approach that is taught in the traineeship program for example, Cognitive based technique , Body based techniques.

7. Self Care of a therapist : As mental health experts in the process of healing others who are emotionally distressed can impact a therapist's mental health too. The module will educate them about ways to prioritise their mental health, self-care framework, attaining key capacities of their real self etc.

8. Ethical issues : This module will include ethical issues in therapy sessions for example confidentiality, breaching the contract, fee issues etc. It will also include rupture in therapy sessions ( misdiagnosis , transference and countertransference, misunderstanding between therapist and a client, managing dependency on therapist.)
